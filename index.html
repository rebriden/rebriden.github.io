We are the Abbington Alligators

About the team

Founded in 1999, the Abbington Alligators swim team is part of the southern league of the Tarheel Swim Association (TSA). The team is composed of swimmers ages 4 to 18, and competes each summer against other neighborhood swim teams. Our goal is not only to show kids the fun of swimming and competitive sports, but to also teach them important life skills that will help them succeed both in and out of the pool.
